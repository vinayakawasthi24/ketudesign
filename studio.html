Who We Are

Where Workplace Strategy Meets Architectural Drama.

Ketu Studio is an elite, turnkey commercial interior design agency representing the intersection of psychological workflow engineering and dramatic spatial aesthetics.

We believe an office should never be a neutral containers for bodies. It is a critical machine that actively dictates corporate culture, projects market dominance, and shapes worker productivity. We replace the generic white drywalls of corporate leases with rich natural stones, structured metallic lines, custom lighting matrices, and environmental acoustics.

Our multidisciplinary team of designers, engineers, construction managers, and workplace psychologists control the entire process from conceptual zoning to final material placement. Operating across India, we have created workspaces for industry titans that elevate physical attendance from an obligation to a premium lifestyle experience.
